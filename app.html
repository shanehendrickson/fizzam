FIZZAM!:
A Coding Project

One hundred bottles of FizzBuzz on the wall.
Take one down, loop it around...
This project uses a loop to print out the numbers 1 to 100. However, it will:
  • Replace any number divisible by your chosen FIZZ value with "FIZZ"
  • Replace any number divisble by your chosen BUZZ value with "BUZZ"
  • Replace any number divisible by the sum of your chosen FIZZ and BUZZ values with "GAZZ"
  • Combine any of the above possibilities that are divisible by more than one of those numbers
  • Unless it's divisible by all three, in which case it gets FIZZAMED!

Instructions

Enter a Fizz value and a Buzz value.
Press the button and the values will appear.
